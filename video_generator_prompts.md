# AI Video Generator: Master Prompts & Scripts

You can use these prompts in modern AI Video Generators (like **InVideo AI**, **HeyGen**, **Synthesia**, or **Fliki**) to generate a presentation video for **EventSnap** automatically.

---

## 1. 🚀 Master Prompt for All-in-One Generators (e.g., InVideo AI / Fliki)

Copy and paste this prompt directly into an AI video generator tool to create the full presentation:

```text
Create a 90-second, high-tech, modern product showcase video for "EventSnap" - an AI-powered college event platform. 

The video must have:
- Tone: Dynamic, energetic, premium, and futuristic (target audience: college students and event organizers).
- Voiceover: Energetic, professional, and clear male or female voice.
- Background Music: Uplifting, fast-paced electronic/ambient synth track.
- Visual Style: Sleek dark mode interfaces, glowing neon overlays (purple, blue, pink), and clean typography animations.

Storyboard & Content flow:
1. Intro (0-15s): Introduce EventSnap as the next-generation event tech for campuses. Hook: "Tired of scrolling through thousands of photos to find yours?"
2. Core Feature 1 - AI Face Search (15-35s): Show a student uploading a selfie and the AI instantly retrieving all matching photos from a gallery. Highlight "DeepFace verification".
3. Core Feature 2 - Live Streaming (35-55s): Show an event organizer broadcasting HD live video/audio from their camera directly to attendees in real time with interactive emoji reactions.
4. Core Feature 3 - AI Auto-Tagging & Reels (55-75s): Show CLIP AI classifying uploaded photos with tags and MoviePy stitching top photos into a dynamic music-synced reel.
5. Outro (75-90s): CTA to sign up, join the campus community, and relive memories.
```
